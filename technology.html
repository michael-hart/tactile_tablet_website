Expanding Wax. Our means of display.

Our system relies on the thermal properties of paraffin wax. When the wax is heated to around 50Â°C, it expands by up to 15%. The wax is placed inside a small chamber covered by a flexible membrane. When the wax expands, the membrane will rise up, creating a Braille dot.

Smart Control. Affordable. Dynamic.

Our system is controlled by a Raspberry Pi, a small, affordable computer.

Cost Effective. Standard industry techniques.

Our revolutionary design is produced using a single printed circuit board. We are using surface mounted resistors on the board to heat up the wax. These will sit alongside the control circuit on the same board. This makes our design much more cost effective to produce than the cantilever alternatives. These savings can then be passed on to the customer, resulting in an affordable Braille device.
