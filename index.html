A New Format Of E-Book. Books for the visually impaired.

Only 4% of English literature is converted to Braille. The Orion tablet will convert digital text into Braille, allowing the reader to upload any digital text, such as a newspaper article or an E-Book, and read it with ease.

Scalable Technology. Scalable to an unlimited screen size.

Unlike existing cantilever based displays, paraffin actuators don’t take up any space beyond the edge of the Braille dots, meaning that infinitely many dots can be placed next to each other and so the display can be of any size instead of a single row allowed by cantilevers.

Portable. The Orion tablet can be taken anywhere.

No more will the visually impaired be constrained to paper books or using heavy equipment powered from the mains. The Orion Tablet is portable and can be used anywhere.
